6/22/2020 99 day of the COVID-19 epidemy in Ukraine Developed by: Sergiy Chornyy Kharkiv, Ukraine
In this research used prediction models based on pandemic equations by William Ogilvy Kermack and A. G. McKendrick (https://doi.org/10.1098/rspa.1927.0118).
Their model was tuned up to official COVID-19 statistics from Ukrainian Ministry of Health(https://moz.gov.ua/article/news/operativna-informacija-pro-poshirennja-koronavirusnoi-infekcii-2019-ncov-1).
At first was predicted: epidemic peak - on 48th day (5/2/2020), end of the epidemia - on 130-th day (7/23/2020), maximum of confirmed infected people - 23328 persons.
But after after weakening quarantine requirements at 05/25/2020, the situation worsened and further develops
according to the Swedish, Polish, Canada scenarios. We have a danger to get much more infected and death people.
It need immediatelly at least introduce mandatory wearing of masks by everyone and strong control of this.
Graphs above are the results of autor's own scientific research, reflects his point of view and are not official data of any Governmental institutions of Ukraine.
