5/15/2020 61 day of the COVID-19 epidemy in Ukraine Developed by: Sergiy Chornyy Kharkiv, Ukraine
In this research used prediction models based on pandemic equations by William Ogilvy Kermack and A. G. McKendrick (https://doi.org/10.1098/rspa.1927.0118).
Their model adopted to official COVID-2019 statistics from Ukrainian Ministry of Health.
Expected epidemic peak is its 48th day (5/2/2020) and expected end of the epidemia is its 130-th day (7/23/2020).
Expected maximum of registered infected people by this model is about 23328 persons.
Peak when daily registered infected people will be more 500 is 5/5/2020 with maximum about 575 per day summary for Ukraine.
Model will be dynamically changed in accordance to new statistics data.
Graphs above are the results of autor's own scientific research, reflects his point of view and are not official data of any Governmental institutions of Ukraine.
