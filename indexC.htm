Comparison of COVID-2019 in Ukraine and other countries

Epidemy in South Korea is close to end and can be used as example of COVID-2019 Epidemy in local population to comparison with Ukrainian data.
Total infection speed during COVID-2019 in S.Korea can be expressed as sum of 4 infection waves, peaks of which shifted in time on 16, 35, 47 and 60 days
from the first day of Epydemy. Korean experience shows how important is to wait for the end of the 1st infection wave before changing
quarantine limitations to avoid 2nd wave and other infectrion peaks.
It can be seen, that the best case for Ukraine is at least to follow Korean scenario or in the perfect case complete on its 1st infection wave.
As Ukraine is in Europian Region, it is also important to compare its infection data with main Europian countries, that are on peak or close to the end
of the COVID-2019 epidemy to understand possible COVID-2019 scenario.
Important conclusion of this comparison is that COVID-2019 demonstrates multiwave (3-5) passing epidemy.
Typical shift betwen infection waves is about 14-16 days or evel less.
