Comparison of COVID-2019  in Ukraine and other countries

Epidemy in China Hubei (CHHU) and South Korea is close to end and can be used now as examples of COVID-2019 Epidemy   to comparison with Ukrainian data.
As can be seen, total infection speed during COVID-2019  can be expressed as sum of a few infection waves, peaks of which shifted in time
from the first day of Epydemy.  Chineese and Korean experience shows how important is to wait for the end of the 1st infection wave before changing 
 quarantine limitations to avoid 2nd and other infectrion peaks. 
It can be seen, that the best case for Ukraine was at least to follow Korean scenario and  in the perfect case  complete on its 1st infection wave.
As Ukraine is in Europian Region, it is also important to compare its infection data with main Europian countries, that are on peak or close to the  end 
of the COVID-2019 epidemy to understand possible COVID-2019 scenario.
Important conclusion of this comparison is that COVID-2019 demonstrates multiwave (3-10) passing epidemy.
Typical shift betwen infection waves is about 5-16 days or even less.
