Comparison of COVID-19 in Ukraine and other countries

Epidemy of COVID-2019 in China Hubei (CHHU), South Korea and some other countries are close to the end and can be used as examples to comparison with Ukrainian data.
As can be seen, total infection speed (daily) during COVID-19 can be expressed as sum of a few infection waves, peaks of which shifted in time
from the first day of Epydemy. Chineese and Korean experience shows how important is to wait for the end of the 1st infection wave before changing
quarantine limits to avoid 2nd and other infectrion peaks.
It can be seen, that the best case for Ukraine was at least to follow Korean scenario and in the perfect case complete on its 1st infection wave.
As Ukraine is in Europian Region, it is also important to compare its infection data with main Europian countries, that are on peak or close to the end
of the COVID-19 epidemy to understand possible scenarios.
Important conclusion of this comparison is that COVID-19 demonstrates multiwave (3-10) passing epidemy.
Typical shift betwen infection waves is about 5-16 days or even less.
