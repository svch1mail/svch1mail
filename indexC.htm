Comparison of COVID-2019  models for Ukrainian and South Korean Epydemy
Epydemy in South Korea is close to end and can be used as example of COVID-2019 Epydemy in local population to comparison with Ukrainian data.
Total infection speed during COVID-2019 in S.Korea can be expressed as sum of 4 infection waves, peaks of which shifted in time on 16, 35, 47 and 60 days
from the first day of Epydemy. It can be seen, that the best case for Ukraine is at least to follow Korean scenario or in the best case
complete on its 1st infection wave.
