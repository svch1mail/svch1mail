Comparison of COVID-19 in Ukraine and the other countries

Epidemy in China Hubei(CHHU), South Korea, in some Europian countries are close to the end and can be used now as examples of COVID-19 Epidemy to comparison with Ukrainian data and take into account their experience.
As can be seen, total infection speed during COVID-2019 can be expressed as sum of a few infection waves, peaks of which shifted in time
from the first day of Epydemy. Chineese and Korean experience shows how important is to wait for the end of the 1st infection wave before changing
quarantine limitations to avoid 2nd and other infectrion peaks.
It can be seen, that the best case for Ukraine was at least to follow Korean scenario and in the perfect case complete on its 1st infection wave.
As Ukraine is in the Europian Region, it is also important to compare its infection data with main Europian countries, that are on peak or close to the end
of the COVID-19 epidemy to understand possible scenarios.
Important conclusion of this comparison is that COVID-2019 demonstrates multiwave (>3) passing epidemy.
Typical shift betwen infection waves is about 5-16 days or even less.
